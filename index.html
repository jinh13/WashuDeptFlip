Impressive Undergraduate Programs

The undergraduate program has three areas of focus: literature/theory/history; sexuality/the body/health; global and transnational feminist and gender studies; and critical race in gender and sexuality studies.

Outstanding Graduate Programs

Graduates will be given both disciplinary and interdisciplinary scholarly and teaching expertise, along with a rich repertoire of pedagogical skills. With experience working in a collegial and supportive academic unit, students will be able to emulate these skills in their future working environments.
